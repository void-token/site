VOID TOKEN

All tokens have been distributed for free.

What is a VoidToken? An experiment where free tokens are distributed but they lose 5% on each transfer.

Send them on to someone else and watch as the supply is affected. This is an experiement to test tokens

that deflate in supply when transferred. Void was freely sent out. Don't expect the Void you receive to be

worth any actual money or value, these are just tokens for what amounts to an interesting game/experiment.
